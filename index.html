Welcome to Bat Buster!

Saddle up cowboy! As dusk settles over the dusty town, an ancient curse has awakened a swarm of bloodthirsty bats, terrorizing the innocent folks. It's up to you to become the ultimate Bat Buster, wielding your trusty revolver, shotgun, and state-issued bazooka to protect the town from these winged menaces. With your quick draw skills and nerves of steel, let the showdown begin as you face off against the darkness and restore peace to the Wild West once and for all!
